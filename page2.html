This Eid is different.
Because of you.

Every Eid has its own feeling. But this one — this one I will remember for a long time. Not because of anything big. Because of something quiet: the fact that you exist in my life, and I get to celebrate this day knowing that.

There is a kind of peace that comes from knowing someone genuinely has your back — not loudly, not with grand gestures — just quietly, consistently, really.

🌸   that's what you are to me

You work hard in ways most people don't see. You carry things quietly. You show up for the people you love without making it a big deal. On this Eid — someone sees all of that. And is grateful for it.

May this Eid bring you the kind of happiness you give to others. The quiet kind. The real kind. The kind that lasts. 🌙

— Waqas   🌸
