· a few honest words ·

Faiqa — this Eid, I don't just want to say Eid Mubarak.

I want you to know that you are someone I make dua for — quietly, genuinely, every single day. Not because I have to. Because you matter to me in a way that's hard to explain.

✦

I see how hard you work. I see what you carry. I see the girl who stays up late and still shows up — for her dreams, for the people she loves.

You deserve someone who notices that. Someone who checks on you without being asked. Someone who says "khayal rakhna" and genuinely, truly means it.

I want to be that person — not just on Eid — but on every ordinary day that comes after this one.

InshaaAllah, one day I will stand in front of your family and ask for your hand — the right way, the halal way, the way you deserve.

Until that day — this dua, this moment, these words — all of it is only for you. 🌙

— Waqas  ·  hamesha ✦
